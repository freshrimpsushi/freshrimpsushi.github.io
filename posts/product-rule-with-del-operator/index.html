공식

**델 연산자가 포함된 곱셈규칙 **그래디언트(gradient, 기울기) (a) $\nabla{(fg)}=f\nabla{g}+g\nabla{f}$

(b)** $\nabla(\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}) = \mathbf{A} \times (\nabla \times \mathbf{B}) + \mathbf{B} \times (\nabla \times \mathbf{A})+(\mathbf{A} \cdot \nabla)\mathbf{B}+(\mathbf{B} \cdot \nabla) \mathbf{A}$**다이버전스(divergence, 발산) **(c)** $\nabla \cdot (f\mathbf{A}) = f(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A}) + \mathbf{A} \cdot (\nabla f)$

(d)** $\nabla \cdot (\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B}) = \mathbf{B} \cdot (\nabla \times \mathbf{A}) - \mathbf{A} \cdot (\nabla \times \mathbf{B})$**컬(curl, 회전) **(e)** $\nabla \times (f\mathbf{A}) = f(\nabla \times \mathbf{A}) - \mathbf{A} \times (\nabla f)$

(f)** $\nabla \times (\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B}) = (\mathbf{B} \cdot \nabla)\mathbf{A} - (\mathbf{A} \cdot \nabla)\mathbf{B} + \mathbf{A} (\nabla \cdot \mathbf{B}) - \mathbf{B} (\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A})$

증명 전반에서 아인슈타인 노테이션을 쓰고 있으니 헷갈리지 않게 주의하자. 즉 한 변에 같은 인덱스가 두 번 나오면 다음과 같은 의미이다. $$ x_{i}y_{i}=\sum \limits_{i=1}^{3} x_{i}y_{i}=x_{1}y_{1}+x_{2}y_{2}+x_{3}y_{3} $$ 또한 크로네커 델타와 레비-치비타 심볼을 사용하는데 익숙해야하고 그 둘 사이에 관계에 대해서 알고 있어야 증명을 따라가기 쉽다.

증명 (a)

$$ \begin{align*} \nabla(fg) &= \dfrac{\partial (fg)}{\partial x} \hat{\mathbf{x}}+\dfrac{\partial (fg)}{\partial y} \hat{\mathbf{y}} +\dfrac{\partial (fg)}{\partial z} \hat{\mathbf{z}} \\ &= \left( g\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} + f\dfrac{\partial g}{\partial x} \right) \hat{\mathbf{x}} +\left( g\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} + f\dfrac{\partial g}{\partial y} \right) \hat{\mathbf{y}} + \left( g\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z} + f\dfrac{\partial g}{\partial z} \right) \hat{\mathbf{z}} \\ &= g\left( \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}\hat{\mathbf{x}} +\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}\hat{\mathbf{y}} + \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z}\hat{\mathbf{z}} \right) + f\left( \dfrac{\partial g}{\partial x}\hat{\mathbf{x}} +\dfrac{\partial g}{\partial y}\hat{\mathbf{y}} + \dfrac{\partial g}{\partial z}\hat{\mathbf{z}} \right) \\ &= g\nabla f+ f\nabla g \end{align*} $$

■

증명 (b)

좌변을 그대로 계산해보면 다음과 같다. $$ \begin{align*} \nabla \left( \mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B} \right) &= \frac{ \partial \left( \mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B} \right)}{ \partial x_{1}}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{1}+\frac{ \partial \left( \mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B} \right)}{ \partial x_{2}}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{2}+\frac{ \partial \left( \mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B} \right)}{ \partial x_{3}}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{3} \\ &= \sum \limits_{i=1}^{3} \frac{ \partial \left( \mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B} \right)}{ \partial x_{i}}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i} \\ &= \sum \limits_{i=1}^{3} \frac{ \partial \left( \sum _{j=1}^{3}A_{j}B_{j} \right)}{ \partial x_{i}}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i} \\ &= \sum \limits_{i=1}^{3}\sum \limits_{j=1}^{3} \frac{ \partial \left( A_{j}B_{j} \right)}{ \partial x_{i}}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i} \end{align*} $$ 이를 아인슈타인노테이션으로 간단하게 표기하면 다음과 같다. $$ \nabla (\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}) = \frac{\partial(A_jB_j)}{\partial x_i}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i}=\frac{\partial A_j}{\partial x_i} B_j\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i}+A_{j} \frac{\partial B_j}{\partial x_i} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i} $$ 다시 **크로네커 델타** 를 사용하여 위 식을 $X_iY_i=X_iY_j\delta_{ij}$와 같이 표현하면 다음과 같다.

$$ \begin{align*} &&\frac{\partial A_j}{\partial x_i} B_j\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i}+A_{j} \frac{\partial B_j}{\partial x_i} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i} &= \color{blue}{\delta_{jm}}\frac{ \partial \color{blue}{A_{j}}}{ \partial x_{i}} \color{blue}{B_{m}} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i} + \color{blue}{\delta_{jm} A_{m}}\frac{ \partial \color{blue}{B_{j}} }{ \partial x_{i} }\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{i} \\ && &=\color{red}{\delta_{il}}\color{blue}{\delta_{jm}}\frac{ \color{red}{\partial} \color{blue}{A_{j}}}{ \color{red}{\partial x_{i}} } \color{blue}{B_{m}} \color{red}{\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l}} + \color{red}{\delta_{il}}\color{blue}{\delta_{jm} A_{m}} \color{red}{\frac{ \partial \color{blue}{B_{j}} }{ \partial x_{i} }} \color{red}{\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l}} \\ && &=\color{red}{\delta_{jl}}\color{blue}{\delta_{jm}} \left( \color{red}{\frac{ \partial \color{blue}{A_{j}}}{ \partial x_{i} }} \color{blue}{B_{m}}\color{red} {\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l}} + \color{blue}{A_{m}}\color{red}{\frac{ \partial \color{blue}{B_{j}} }{ \partial x_{i} } \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} }\right) \\ \implies && \nabla \left( \mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} \right) &=\delta_{jl}\delta_{jm} \left( \frac{ \partial A_{j} }{ \partial x_{i} } B_{m} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} + A_{m}\frac{ \partial B_{j} }{ \partial x_{i} } \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} \right) \end{align*} $$

또한 $\epsilon_{ijk} \epsilon_{klm} = \delta_{il} \delta_{jm} - \delta_{im} \delta_{jl}$이므로 위 식을 다음과 같이 전개할 수 있다. $$ \begin{align*} \nabla(\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}) &= (\epsilon_{ijk} \epsilon_{klm} + \delta_{im} \delta_{jl}) \left(\frac {\partial A_{j}}{\partial x_{i}}B_{m} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} + A_{m} \frac{\partial B_{j}}{\partial x_{i}}\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l}\right) \\ &= \epsilon_{ijk} \epsilon_{klm } \frac{\partial A_{j}}{\partial x_{i}}B_{m} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} + \epsilon_{ijk} \epsilon_{klm} A_{m} \frac{\partial B_{j}}{\partial x_{i}} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} + \delta_{im} \delta_{jl} \frac{\partial A_{j}}{\partial x_{i}} B_{m} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} + \delta_{im} \delta_{jl}A_{m} \frac{\partial B_{j}}{\partial x_{i}} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{l} \end{align*} $$ 여기서 레비-치비타 심볼의 정의에 의해 $\epsilon_{ijk} \dfrac{\partial A_{j}}{\partial x_{i}}=(\nabla \times \mathbf{A})_{k}$, $\epsilon_{ijk} \dfrac {\partial B_{j}}{\partial x_{i}}=(\nabla \times \mathbf{B})_{k}$ 이므로 $$ \begin{align*} \nabla (\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}) &= \epsilon _{ klm }(\nabla \times \mathbf{A})_{ k }B_{ m }\hat { \mathbf{e}_{ l } }+\epsilon _{ klm }A_{ m }(\nabla \times \mathbf{B})_{ k }\hat { \mathbf{e}_{ l } }+\frac { \partial A_{ j } }{ \partial x_{ i } }B_{ i }\hat { e_{ j} }+A_{ i }\frac { \partial B_{ j } }{ \partial x_{ i } }\hat { \mathbf{e}_{ j } } \\ &= \mathbf{B}\times (\nabla \times \mathbf{A})+\mathbf{A} \times (\nabla \times \mathbf{B})+(\mathbf{B} \cdot \nabla )\mathbf{A}+(\mathbf{A} \cdot \nabla )\mathbf{B} \end{align*} $$

증명 (c)​

$$ \begin{align*} \nabla \cdot (f \mathbf{A}) &= \delta _{ ij }\nabla _{ i }(fA_{ j }) \\ &= \delta _{ ij }(\nabla _{ i }f)A_{ j }+\delta _{ ij }f(\nabla _{ i }A_{ j }) \\ &= (\nabla _{ i }f)A_{ i }+f(\nabla _{ i }A_{ i }) \\ &= (\nabla f)\cdot \mathbf{A}+f(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A}) \end{align*}
$$

■

증명 (d)

$$ \begin{align*} \nabla \cdot (\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B}) &= \delta _{ ij }\nabla _{ i }(\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B})_{ j } \\ &=\delta _{ ij }\nabla _{ i }(\epsilon _{ jkl }A_{ k }B_{ l }) \\ &=\delta _{ ij }\epsilon _{ jkl }\nabla _{ i }(A_{ k }B_{ l }) \\ &=\delta _{ ij }\epsilon _{ jkl }(\nabla _{ i }A_{ k })B_{ l }+\delta _{ ij }\epsilon _{ jkl }A_{ k }(\nabla _{ i }B_{ l }) \\ &= (\epsilon _{ jkl }\nabla _{ j }A_{ k })B_{ l }+A_{ k }(\epsilon _{ jkl }\nabla _{ j }B_{ l }) \\ &=(\nabla \times A)_{ l }B_{ l }-A_{ k }(\nabla \times B)_{ k } \\ &=(\nabla \times \mathbf{A})\cdot \mathbf{B}-\mathbf{A}\cdot (\nabla \times \mathbf{B}) \end{align*}
$$

■

증명 (e)​

$$ \begin{align*} \nabla \times (f\mathbf{A}) &=\epsilon _{ ijk }\nabla _{ i }(fA_{ j })\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} \\ &= \epsilon _{ ijk }(\nabla _{ i }f)A_{ j }\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k}+\epsilon _{ ijk }f(\nabla _{ i }A_{ j })\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} \\ &= (\nabla f)\times \mathbf{A}+f\epsilon _{ ijk }(\nabla _{ i }A_{ j })\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} \\ &= (\nabla f)\times \mathbf{A}+f(\nabla \times \mathbf{A}) \\ &= f(\nabla \times \mathbf{A})-\mathbf{A} \times (\nabla f) \end{align*} $$

■

증명 (f)

아인슈타인 노테이션에 익숙하지 않다면 증명을 따라가는데 힘들 것이다. $$ \begin{align*} \nabla \times (\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{B}) &= \epsilon_{ijk} \nabla_{i} \left(\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{B}\right)_{j} \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} = \epsilon_{ijk} \nabla_{i} (\epsilon_{jlm} A_{l} B_{m})\hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} \\ &= \epsilon_{ijk} \epsilon_{jlm} \nabla_{i} (A_{l} B_{m}) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k}
\\ &= \epsilon_{jki} \epsilon_{jlm} \left[ B_{m}(\nabla_{i} A_{l}) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} + A_{l} (\nabla_{i} B_{m}) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} \right] \\ &= (\delta_{kl} \delta_{im} - \delta_{km} \delta_{il} ) [ B_{m} (\nabla_{i} A_{l} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} + A_{l} ( \nabla_{i} B_{m} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} ] \\ &= \delta_{kl} \delta_{im} B_{m} ( \nabla_{i} A_{l}) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} - \delta_{km} \delta_{il} B_{m} (\nabla_{ i} A_{l} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} + \delta_{kl} \delta_{im} A_{l} ( \nabla_{i} B_{m} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} - \delta_{km} \delta_{il} A_{l} ( \nabla_{i} B_{m} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} \\ &= B_{i} ( \nabla_{i} A_{k}) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} - B_{k} (\nabla_{i} A_{i} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} + A_{k} ( \nabla_{i} B_{i} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} - A_{i} ( \nabla_{i} B_{k} ) \hat{\mathbf{e}}_{k} \\ &= (\mathbf{B}\cdot \nabla )\mathbf{A}-(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A})\mathbf{B}+\mathbf{A}(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{B})-(\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla )\mathbf{B} \\ &= (\mathbf{B}\cdot \nabla )\mathbf{A}-(\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla )\mathbf{B}+\mathbf{A}(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{B})-\mathbf{B}(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A}) \end{align*} $$ 네번째 줄은 $\epsilon_{jki} \epsilon_{jlm} = \delta_{kl} \delta_{im} - \delta_{km} \delta_{il}$에 의해 성립한다. 일곱번째 줄은 아인슈타인 노테이션에 의해 성립한다.
